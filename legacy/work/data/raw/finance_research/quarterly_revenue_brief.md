# 2025 Quarterly Revenue Brief

The 2025 structured finance dataset shows quarter-over-quarter revenue expansion across East China, North China, and South China.

- 2025Q1 revenue baseline: East China 1.20M, North China 0.98M, South China 1.10M.
- 2025Q2 revenue: East China 1.45M, North China 1.21M, South China 1.39M.
- 2025Q3 revenue: East China 1.62M, North China 1.30M, South China 1.70M.
- Gross margin improves from 0.39-0.42 in Q1 to 0.41-0.46 in Q3.
- South China posts the strongest Q1-to-Q3 revenue growth, while East China remains the largest revenue base.

2025 年结构化财务样例显示，华东、华北、华南三个区域的营收按季度持续增长。
华南在 Q1 到 Q3 之间的营收增幅最高，华东保持最大营收体量，毛利率也从 Q1 的 0.39-0.42 提升到 Q3 的 0.41-0.46。
