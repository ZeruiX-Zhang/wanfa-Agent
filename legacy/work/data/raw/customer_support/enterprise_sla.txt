Enterprise customer P1 incidents require a first response within 30 minutes.

For P1 tickets, the support team must acknowledge the issue within 30 minutes, assign an owner, and notify the duty manager if the incident affects production users.

企业客户 P1 问题要求在 30 分钟内首次响应。
支持团队需要在 30 分钟内确认问题、分配负责人，并在影响生产用户时通知值班经理。
